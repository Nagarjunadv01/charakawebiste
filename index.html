Who We Are

CHARAKA is designed to enable translation from clinical need to real-world impact.

What CHARAKA will enable
  • Innovation from problem identification to deployment
  • Access to shared labs and testing facilities (planned)
  • Clinical validation networks (being established)
  • Pathways for government pilots and scale
Explore Vision
CHARAKA is a Government of Karnataka initiative being established as a translational platform for healthcare innovation. It will bring together clinicians, engineers, researchers, startups, and policymakers to accelerate the journey from idea to adoption. The Centre of Excellence (CoE) in MedTech, HealthTech & Assistive Tech, a section 8, not for profit, established by the Government of Karnataka represents a strategic public investment to address one of India’s most pressing and complex challenges: translating clinical needs, scientific innovation into scalable, affordable, accessible and deployable technologies.
