CHARAKA Startup Support Platform (Planned)

What it will offer:
A structured pathway for early-stage HealthTech and MedTech startups to move from concept to validated solution.

01

Designed For

  • ● Early-stage startups
  • ● Innovators with prototypes
  • ● Academic spin-offs
02

Planned Support Areas

  • ● Mentorship (clinical, technical, business)
  • ● Access to prototyping and testing infrastructure (planned)
  • ● Connections to hospitals and validation partners
  • ● Investor and funding ecosystem access
03

Expected Outcomes

  • ● Stronger, validation-ready startups
  • ● Reduced time from prototype to pilot
